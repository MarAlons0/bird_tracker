Welcome to Mario's Bird Tracker

This application helps you track and discover bird sightings in your area, by using reports in the eBird for the past 7 days and leveraging Claude AI for summarization. Here's how it works:

  • Map: View bird sightings on an interactive map of your selected location.
  • AI Analysis: Get insights about bird populations and trends in your area.
  • Newsletter: Receive email updates about new bird sightings in your area.
